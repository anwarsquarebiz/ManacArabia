Conduit Fittings

Conduit fittings play a crucial role in establishing connections between conduits or linking conduits to electrical boxes. These fittings come in various metals and are designed based on the type of connections and the conduit diameter. Khayal Energy Trading provides a range of exceptional electrical conduit fittings tailored to meet your diverse needs, all at reasonable prices.

Explore our diverse selection of conduit fittings:

RGS Fitting:

This involves the use of heavy-duty galvanized steel tubing, installed with threaded fittings. Primarily designed for outdoor use, RGS fittings offer structural support for your electrical cables and equipment.

EMT Conduit Fittings:

Zinc-plated and constructed from steel, EMT conduit fittings feature insulating bushings. These fittings use either set screws or compression rings to secure EMT pipes, ensuring reliable and insulated connections.

Liquid Tight Conduit Fittings:

Made from malleable iron, steel, or nylon and zinc-plated to prevent corrosion, these fittings are certified for both wet and dry locations. Liquid Tight Conduit Fittings provide a versatile solution suitable for various environmental conditions.
