Tools Accessories

Manac Arabia Establishment is an esteemed supplier of exceptional quality hand tools, power tools, and accessories. All tools are designed using top-grade material under the supervision of expert quality controllers. Given below are some common tools and accessories that we offer to our customers.

Grinding Discs:

They are among the most common tools used in construction projects. They help to remove paint and rust effectively. They are also used for the finishing and preparation of stone and metal surfaces.

Cutting Wheels:

These wheels are capable of sawing through nearly every material known to man. They operate at a faster speed and make narrow, precise cuts at 90-degree angles.

Drill Press:

It is used for making holes in hard substances. The drill is held on a rotating axis and is gorged into the workpiece, which is usually fastened on a resting table.

Cable Cutters:

These tools help to properly cut wire or cables with minimal damage to the insulation or conductor inside the wire. They help improve the quality of electrical connections significantly.
