Weatherproof Boxes

Ensuring the safety and protection of electrical connections in outdoor settings is crucial. Manac Arabia Establishment provides top-quality weatherproof covers and boxes designed to withstand diverse climatic conditions. These robust and durable outdoor solutions come with watertight exterior covers.

Available Shapes and Sizes:

Square Box:

Also known as a "double-gang box," it accommodates two devices, featuring a combination of outlet and switch.

Rectangular Box:

Houses a single electrical switch or outlet and offers protection from the elements with gaskets and watertight covers.

Round Box:

Typically 1/2 inch or 3/4 inch deep, used for lightweight ceiling or wall-mounted light fixtures and alarms.

Octagonal Box:

Designed for housing lightweight fixtures or safety devices in the ceiling, these metal boxes come in standard sizes of 4 x 4 inches, with depths of 1 1/4, 1 1/2, and 2 1/8 inches.
