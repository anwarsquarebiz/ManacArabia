Cable Accessories Management System

A cable management system for accessories facilitates the organized arrangement of cables connected to electrical devices, contributing to the creation of a visually uncluttered work environment. Manac Arabia Establishment specializes in the design and manufacturing of a diverse range of cable accessories tailored to meet your specific requirements.

Diverse Applications of Cable Accessories:

MV Cable Lug

Our MV Cable Lug is designed to facilitate the connection of cables to the terminals of electrical devices with a voltage rating ranging from 1000V to 30KV.

LV Cable Lug:

Specifically crafted for medium voltage applications up to 11kV, our LV Cable Lug is constructed from copper tubes engineered to optimize both electrical conductivity and mechanical strength.

Cable Glands:

Our Cable Glands play a crucial role in attaching and securing the ends of electric cables and devices. They provide functions such as earthing, grounding, insulation, bonding, and strain relief.

Cable Cleats:

The Cable Cleats we offer are instrumental in securing cables, strategically installed at intervals along the length of the cables to ensure stability.

Cable Ties:

For bundling cables and wires together to maintain organization and minimize the risk of damage, our Cable Ties provide a reliable solution.

At Manac Arabia Establishment, our cable accessories management system is not just about functionality; it's about providing tailored solutions that contribute to an efficient and well-organized workspace.
